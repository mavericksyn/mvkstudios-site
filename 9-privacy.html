Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026

This policy explains how MVK Studios collects, uses, stores and protects your personal information when you engage with our services, visit our website or get in touch with our team.

01 Introduction

MVK Studios is committed to protecting the privacy of every founder, team and visitor we work with. This Privacy Policy describes the kinds of personal information we handle and the steps we take to keep it secure, in line with the Australian Privacy Principles under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

By using our website or engaging our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy. If you do not agree with how we handle personal information, please do not provide it to us or continue using our services.

02 Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that is reasonably necessary to deliver our services and operate our business. The information we collect depends on how you interact with us.

Information you provide

  • Contact details such as your name, business name, email address and phone number
  • Information shared during a strategy call, engagement or written correspondence
  • Business context, tools and workflow details relevant to a project

Information collected automatically

  • Device and browser information, including your IP address and operating system
  • Usage data describing how you navigate and interact with our website
  • Cookies and similar technologies, as described in our Cookie Policy

03 How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect to operate, maintain and improve our services, and to communicate with you in a relevant and timely way.

  • To respond to enquiries and provide the services you have requested
  • To scope, deliver and manage client engagements
  • To send administrative messages, updates and service communications
  • To understand how our website is used and to improve its performance
  • To meet our legal, accounting and regulatory obligations

We will not use your personal information for a purpose unrelated to those described above without first obtaining your consent, unless we are required or authorised to do so by law.

04 Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, understand usage patterns and improve your experience. Some cookies are essential for the website to function, while others help us measure and refine our content.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. For full detail on the cookies we use and how to manage them, please read our Cookie Policy.

05 Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with trusted third parties only where it is necessary to deliver our services or where we are required to do so by law.

  • Service providers who support our operations, such as hosting, analytics and email platforms
  • Professional advisers, including accountants and legal counsel, where relevant
  • Regulatory or government bodies where disclosure is required by law

Where we engage third parties, we take reasonable steps to ensure they handle your information consistently with this policy and applicable privacy laws.

06 Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from misuse, interference, loss, unauthorised access, modification or disclosure.

While we work hard to safeguard your information, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we will act promptly to investigate and respond to any suspected data breach in line with our obligations.

07 Your Rights

You have rights in relation to the personal information we hold about you, including the right to access it and request corrections where it is inaccurate or out of date.

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct or update inaccurate information
  • Request that we delete information where we are not required to retain it
  • Withdraw consent to certain communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity before actioning your request.

08 Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, or wish to make a request or complaint about how we handle your personal information, please get in touch.

Email us at hello@mvkstudios.com.au or call 0415 380 648. We aim to respond to all privacy enquiries within a reasonable timeframe.
