Overview

The Situation.

Apex Physio Clinics is a multi-location physiotherapy group operating across four Melbourne suburbs. By mid-2024, they were managing patient bookings, recalls, and follow-ups across spreadsheets, a basic practice management tool, and individual staff email inboxes — none of which talked to each other.

New patient enquiries were being responded to hours after they arrived. Recall campaigns for lapsed patients were manual, infrequent, and untracked. Front desk staff were spending 14+ hours per week on tasks that could — and should — be automated. Apex brought in MVK Studios to fix the infrastructure, not just add another tool.

⚡
The Challenge

Disconnected Systems, Lost Patients

Four locations. No unified patient database. No automated follow-up. Every new lead was manually handled — and most weren't handled fast enough to convert.

🎯
The Solution

One System Across All Four Locations

A unified GoHighLevel CRM with automated booking flows, recall sequences, and real-time reporting — built and live within 5 business days.

Deliverables

What We Built.

Every component below was built, tested, and deployed within the 60-day engagement window. Nothing was templated — each automation was architected around Apex's actual patient journey.

Full Delivery Scope

GoHighLevel CRM setup across all 4 locations
Unified patient contact database & tagging system
Online booking automation with calendar sync
New patient welcome SMS + email sequence
Appointment reminder automation (48h + 2h)
Lapsed patient recall campaign (6-week sequence)
Post-appointment review request automation
Location-specific pipeline reporting dashboard
How We Did It

The Process.

01

Discovery & Workflow Mapping

Two days with the Apex team mapping the current patient journey — from initial enquiry through first appointment, re-booking, and lapse. Identified 11 manual touchpoints that could be automated without losing the personal feel.

02

CRM Architecture & Data Migration

Built the GoHighLevel account structure — four sub-locations, custom fields for patient type and treatment history, and a tag architecture that allows campaigns to target by location, last visit date, and condition type. Migrated 2,400+ existing patient records.

03

Booking & Automation Build

Integrated the online booking form with the CRM pipeline. Built automated confirmation, reminder, and post-visit sequences. Each sequence was A/B tested across SMS and email to optimise open and click-through rates before going live site-wide.

04

Recall Campaign Launch

Deployed a 6-touchpoint recall sequence to 1,100+ lapsed patients — those who hadn't booked in 90+ days. First send achieved a 68% open rate and 220 re-bookings within the first 14 days.

05

Handover & Ongoing Optimisation

Trained the Apex admin team on the CRM dashboard and reporting. Set up monthly optimisation reviews as part of the ongoing Medium Business plan — adjusting sequences, updating tags, and adding new automations as the business grows.
