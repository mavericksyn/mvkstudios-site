What's Included

Everything Your Practice Needs.

Automated Recall Sequences

SMS and email recalls at 3, 6, 9, and 12-month intervals — triggered automatically from your patient database with no manual effort from staff.

Post-Visit Review Requests

Review request SMS fires 4 hours after every completed appointment. Consistent volume of new Google reviews without any team intervention.

Digital New Patient Intake

Online intake forms linked directly to patient records in GoHighLevel — replacing paper forms and manual data entry on arrival.

Appointment Reminders

Automated SMS and email confirmations and 48-hour reminders that reduce no-shows and free reception staff from manual reminder calls.

Lapsed Patient Reactivation

Multi-touch SMS and email campaigns targeting patients who haven't been in for 18+ months — designed to rebook without feeling pushy.

Monthly Reporting Dashboard

Clear monthly reports showing recalls sent, bookings generated, reviews collected, and no-show rate — everything in one view.
