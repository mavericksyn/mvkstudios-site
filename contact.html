FAQ

Common Questions.

How quickly can you get my CRM set up?

+

Most Starter CRM builds are live within 3–5 business days from onboarding. Medium and Enterprise builds vary — typically 1–3 weeks depending on complexity and integration requirements.

Do I need a GoHighLevel account already?

+

No — we handle the setup and provisioning as part of your plan. GoHighLevel is included in all MVK Studios subscription plans, so there's nothing extra to purchase separately.

Is there a minimum contract length?

+

All plans are month-to-month subscriptions — no lock-in contracts. We believe in earning your business every month, not trapping you in a 12-month agreement.

Can I upgrade my plan as my business grows?

+

Yes — our plans are designed with natural upgrade paths. You can move between Small, Medium, and Large tiers at any time, and we'll migrate your existing data and automations as part of the upgrade.

Do you work with businesses outside Melbourne?

+

Absolutely. While we're Melbourne-based, we work with clients across Australia and internationally. Everything we do is delivered digitally, so location is never a barrier.

What happens in the free strategy call?

+

It's a 30-minute conversation where we ask about your business, your current tools, and your goals. At the end, we recommend the plan that fits — or tell you honestly if we're not the right fit.
