Pricing FAQ

Common Questions.

Are the prices shown in AUD?

+

Yes — all prices are in Australian Dollars (AUD) and inclusive of GST where applicable.

Is there a setup fee?

+

No setup fees on any plan. Your first subscription payment covers everything including onboarding, CRM build, and initial automation setup.

Do I need a GoHighLevel account?

+

No. GoHighLevel access is included in all plans. We provision and manage your account as part of the subscription.

Can I cancel at any time?

+

Yes — all plans are month-to-month with no lock-in contracts. Cancel with 30 days' notice at any time.

How quickly am I onboarded?

+

Small Business plans are typically live within 3–5 business days. Medium and Enterprise plans take 1–3 weeks depending on complexity.

Can I upgrade between tiers?

+

Absolutely. Upgrades happen at your next billing cycle. We migrate all your data and automations as part of the transition at no extra cost.
