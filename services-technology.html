Our Process

Clear Process.
No Surprises.

Every engagement follows the same structured approach — so you always know where things stand and what comes next.

01
Discovery & Scope

We map your existing stack, identify the gaps and agree on a clear scope before a single line of code is written. No surprises mid-build.

02
Architecture & Design

We design the full solution — system architecture, data flows, integrations — and get your sign-off before build begins.

03
Build & Test

Development with regular check-ins and a shared staging environment so you see progress throughout, not just at the end.

04
Launch & Support

We handle deployment, provide documentation and stay on hand for the post-launch period. Ongoing support retainers available.
