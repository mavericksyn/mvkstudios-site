What’s Included

Two Phases.
One Clear Output.

The Blueprint covers the first two stages of our engagement model — Diagnose and Design — before any implementation begins.

Phase 01 — Diagnose

Map the System

Weeks 1–2 — Kick-off workshop and async review

  • Business systems map — how work flows through your team today
  • Workflow and handoff audit — where things slow down, drop or break
  • Tool stack review — what you have, what overlaps, what’s missing
  • Team capacity analysis — where the founder and key people are over-stretched
  • Bottleneck identification — root causes, not symptoms
  • Founder leverage review — what only you can do versus what you’re doing out of habit

Phase 02 — Design

Build the Roadmap

Week 2–3 — Synthesis and delivery session

  • Operating model roadmap — how the business should run at your next stage
  • Priority implementation sequence — what to fix first, second and third
  • Delegation and automation plan — what to hand off to people versus tools
  • AI opportunity map — where AI reduces founder load in your specific model
  • Quick wins identification — high-impact, low-effort changes to act on immediately
  • 90-day action plan — the first quarter of work, sequenced and scoped
